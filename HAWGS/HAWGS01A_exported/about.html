Yet another website for diving ...

H.A.W.G.S. = Have Air Wanna Go Scuba

Many years ago I certified as an open water diver in Hawaii and when I returned to Calgary I found that using local diving to practice and improve my skills was a challenge - specifically because I did not know anyone who was a diver or where the divesites were.

Being a new novice diver also presented the problem of what to do at the sites - they certainly donâ€™t have the animals and underwater scenery that I saw in Hawaii!!

However, despite these obstacles, I did extra training with a very cooperative dive shop and learned to dive in interesting and challenging conditions . This also lead me to meet local hard core dive buddies who generally knew where to go diving.

The purpose of this site is to help both novice and experienced divers find dive sites, suggest activities to improve skills and to provide another channel for buddies to meet.

The sign up process is designed to help notify people of events of interest and allow people contact each other for diving - it serves no other purpose.
